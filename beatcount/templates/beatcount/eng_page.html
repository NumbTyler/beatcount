Introduction

　We are doing various research on music. As part of that, this time we will answer a few questions based on your music related abilities. In the experiment of this time, you ask "to find the 1st beat of the 1st measure while listening to the song played from the middle and take the count accurately from there."

About Structure of Music

Before conducting experiments, we will use actual songs to explain the sense of music and beats.

Most of the songs that are released in general, except for classical music etc., are created to change the tune with a certain bar sense based on 8 bars. The video below will be the explanation video. Some songs are made up of simple 8 bar combinations, some are included in 8 bars ⇒ 4 bars ⇒ 8 bars, between 8 bars and 8 bars, 2 bars and 4 bars added more clearly Some songs have parts that change the tune. For the songs used in this experiment, we adopt the song group of both patterns.
